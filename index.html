what is a nucleas?

A nucleas acts as the control center of the cell. It regulates the cell growth and reproduction. The nucleas contains structerescalled chromosomes. Chromosomes contain the cell genes, which determine the charcteristic of the cell.

How dose a nucleas work

By houseing the cell's genome,the nucleas servs both as the repository of genetic information and as the cells control center. DNA replication,transcription, and RNA processing all take place within the nuculeas, with only the final stage of gene expression localized to the cytoplasm.
